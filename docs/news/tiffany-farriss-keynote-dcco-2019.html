Tiffany Farriss to Keynote DCCO 2019

We are thrilled to announce that Tiffany Farriss, CEO of Palantir.net, will be our keynote speaker at DrupalCamp Colorado 2019!

About Tiffany
As CEO of Palantir.net, a Chicago-headquartered Web strategy, design and development team, Tiffany provides project leadership and strategy through consultation, project planning and coaching. She has over 20 years of experience on a wide variety of projects for higher ed, healthcare, cultural and media clients. Tiffany also served as a Director of the Drupal Association from 2009-2017 and as its Treasurer from 2011-2017.

Keynote Abstract
Learning @ Work: With tech still struggling to achieve its diversity and inclusion goals and average job tenure down to less than 3 years, we need to transform how we think about our organizational cultures. How do we create environments that succeed because of the teams, but where that success is not dependent on any one person? How do we align the company and individual interests so that everyone benefits from however much time that they work together? This presentation explores the role that culture and learning have in organizations (as well as open source projects like Drupal) as we work to answer those questions.

Brought to You By
Our Keynote Sponsor, Palantir.net is generously covering the cost of Tiffany’s travel and time to join us at DrupalCamp Colorado. Be sure to track down Nelson or Tiffany at the camp to let them know how much you appreciate their company's generous contribution to making this year’s camp a success.

DrupalCamp Colorado will take place August 2-4, 2019 at the King Center on Auraria Campus in downtown Denver. Join us for three days of summits, trainings, sessions, code sprints and networking with the Southwest Drupal community!
